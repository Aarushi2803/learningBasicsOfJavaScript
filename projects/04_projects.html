Number guessing game

Try and guess a random number between 1 to 100.

You have 6 attempts to guess the correct number.

  • Enter a number in the input field.
  • Click the submit button to check your guess.
  • If your guess is incorrect, you'll get a hint: "Too high!" or "Too low!"
  • Keep guessing until you find the right number or run out of attempts.
  • Win the game by guessing correctly within 6 tries!
